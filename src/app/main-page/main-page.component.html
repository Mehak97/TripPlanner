“Bizarre travel plans are dancing lessons from God.”

BENEFITS

1. You won’t miss out on anything

Making an itinerary will force you to do your research ahead of time. This may be the only time you visit this place, SO you want to be sure you see everything you came to see. Through your research, you might even learn of destinations, festivals or exhibits you may not have known existed.

2. It will help with packing

Having a tentative plan of what places you’re visiting and activities you’ll be participating in helps greatly in efficient packing. Instead of four pairs of shoes, you’ll know that you may not need those hiking boots after all. It will also help when you’re choosing what to wear each day,

3. Budget for your trip over time

Travel is a fulfilling, life-changing investment. Planning for your trip ahead of time enables you and your group members to properly save for your tour and arrange for smaller, more manageable payments over time.
